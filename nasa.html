NASA Hunch Trash Ejection System

Project Writeup | Final Demo

Led team of four students to design and fabricate a trash ejection system for future Mars missions. Team selected as one of four finalists nationwide from hundreds of participants in NASA's HUNCH program.

Designed and prototyped 3 iterations of the Mars Mission Trash Ejection System, managing a 30-element CAD assembly with human error prevention features. Project focused on safety, reliability, and ease of use for long-duration space missions.
